Orientation @ Wits University

Orientation! What's that?

You are about to embark on one of the most exciting and enjoyable phases of your life. To ensure your journey is a success, Wits has developed an orientation programme for you (campus tours, meeting faculty and staff, numerous seminars, etc.)

About your Wits Passport

We have designed a programme to meet your specific needs. As such, it is in your best interest to attend the entire programme. Lookout for events and sessions that require you to get stamped. Participate in various activities and stand a chance to win a Prize.

Welcome Day

Parents and students will get the opportunity to meet the Vice Chancellor and other members of the Senior Executive Team. They will also have the opportunity to engage with the Dean of Students and the SRC President

Student Representative Council

Short for SRC, this body represents students and defends their interests. As a student, SRC wants to get to know you. It cares about your concerns, queries and complaints. Visit SRC on the 2 nd floor at the Matrix. Your SRC cares...
